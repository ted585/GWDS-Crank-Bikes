Giving back with Crank

At Crank Bikes, we are committed to sustainability and giving back to the planet. As a cycle share company, we believe that eco-friendly transportation should go hand in hand with environmental conservation. That’s why we donate 20% of our profits to leading NGOs like WWF and Greenpeace. By supporting these organizations, we help protect wildlife, fund reforestation projects, and drive climate action initiatives. Every time someone rides with us, they’re not just choosing a greener way to travel—they’re also contributing to vital efforts that combat deforestation, ocean pollution, and carbon emissions. Together, we can pedal toward a cleaner, healthier planet for future generations.

A bike for everyone

With extra adjustability on our bikes, we make sure that everyone—no matter their height or riding style—can enjoy a smooth and comfortable ride. Our easily adjustable seats and handlebars mean that whether you're tall, short, or somewhere in between, you can find the perfect fit in seconds. This flexibility allows us to welcome more riders and ensure that our bike share system is truly accessible to all. a cleaner, healthier planet for future generations.

For any occasion

With extra storage on our bikes, we make it easy for riders to use them in any situation—whether they're commuting to work, running errands, or picking up groceries. Our spacious front baskets and secure cargo options give you the freedom to carry bags, laptops, or even a quick shopping haul with ease. By designing our bikes with convenience in mind, we ensure that they’re not just for getting from A to B but for making everyday life easier and easier.
